Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has condemned the terror attack at a school in Peshawar, Pakistan.
“Strongly condemn the cowardly terrorist attack at a school in Peshawar.
It is a senseless act of unspeakable brutality that has claimed lives of the most innocent of human beings – young children in their school.
My heart goes out to everyone who lost their loved ones today.
We share their pain and offer our deepest condolences,” the Prime Minister said.
